IShowSpeed (Darren Jason Watkins Jr.)

Summary

Born Jan 21, 2005

Darren Jason Watkins Jr. (born January 21, 2005), known online as IShowSpeed or Speed, is an American internet personality and online streamer. He is known for his variety and gaming livestreams which showcase his dramatic and energetic behaviour, as well as his IRL streams around the world.
